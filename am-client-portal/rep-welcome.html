Website

Pitch 1: Website ($500, pay only if you like it)

+

"Hey, quick question. You want the good news or the bad news?"

[They respond.]

"Bad news first. You got no website, or the one you have looks like it was built when fish still had dial-up. Right now somebody's googling 'fishing guide near me' and booking with the guy who showed up. That's not you.

Good news, I'll build you a full website, clean, professional, your photos, your rates, your trips, booking form, the whole thing. And you don't pay a dime until you see it and you like it. If you don't like it, I walk away. Zero dollars out of your pocket.

You've got nothing to lose and a full website to gain. You got 10 minutes this week to talk?"

Google Ads

Pitch 2: Google Ads ($500/mo + ad spend)

+

"Quick one for ya. Good news or bad news first?"

[They respond.]

"Bad news. Right now when someone types 'fishing guide [their area]' into Google, your competitors are showing up at the top and they're getting those bookings. You're invisible.

Good news, I run Google Ads for fishing guides. Here's how it works: you only pay Google when someone actually clicks on your ad. Not when they see it, not when they scroll past it. Only when they click. So you're not burning money on people who don't care, you're paying for people who are already looking to book a trip.

I manage the whole thing for five hundred a month, and I'm watching your conversions the whole time. If people are clicking but not booking, I find out why and I fix it. You're not just getting clicks, you're getting bookings.

What does a full charter run you? Because this pays for itself fast. You want to talk numbers for your area?"

SEO

Pitch 3: SEO (Long-term rankings)

+

"Hey, good news or bad news?"

[They respond.]

"Bad news. Google's got no idea you exist. You're on page three or four, which means you might as well not be there. Other guides who've been around online longer are getting found every single day for free while you're not.

Good news, that's fixable. SEO takes 3 to 6 months but once it kicks in you are getting found on Google without paying for ads, every day, permanently. I handle the whole thing.

It's not overnight but it's the best long-term move you can make. You want to know what it would take in your market?"
